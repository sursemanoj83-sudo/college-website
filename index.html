About us

Sai Institute of Technology (Polytechnic) was established in August 2011 by eminent personality of Chh. Sambhaji Nagar MR.J.K JADHAV. It is situated behind Garware Polyester in the vicinity of WOCKHARDT. The aim of the college is to provide quality technical education and instill in the student the practical programmatic approach towards applications of the knowledge.

Shri Sai Institute Of Technology (Polytechnic) intended to provide congenial atmosphere to mould today's youth into diligent, sincere engineers of tomorrow's that he or she can face any challenges undeterred it seeks to inculcate with in depth technical knowledge the sense of purpose and importance of team work and leadership qualities. The college is approved by A.I.C.T.E New Delhi and D.T.E.
